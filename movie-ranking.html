My top 3 Movies

Hum sath sath hain

Hum sath sath hain is a 1999 Indian Hindi-language family drama film directed by Sooraj R. Barjatya and produced by Rajshri Productions. The film stars Salman Khan, Saif Ali Khan, Karishma Kapoor, Tabu, and Reema Lagoo. The story revolves around the lives of a joint family and their relationships with each other.
